================================================================================
COMBAT MECHANICS
================================================================================

Attack Roll = Weapon Skill + D10 + Modifiers
Defense Roll = Weapon/Dodge Skill + D10 + Modifiers

If Attack > Defense:
  Damage = (Attack - Defense) + Strength Bonus + Weapon Damage - Armor

Wound Level:
  - Light: Damage < Constitution
  - Severe: Damage ≥ Constitution
  - Critical: Damage ≥ Constitution × 2

Wound Stacking:
  - 4 Light → 1 Severe
  - 3 Severe → 1 Critical
  - 2 Critical → Death
